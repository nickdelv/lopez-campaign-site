# Project Brief — Lisa Lopez for State Representative

## The Campaign

**Candidate:** Lisa Lopez
**Race:** Massachusetts State Representative, 6th Norfolk District
**District:** Canton, Stoughton, and Avon
**Party:** Democrat
**Cycle:** 2026

Lisa Lopez is a Yale-trained attorney, former chief legal officer at Haemonetics Corporation (25 years), and longtime Canton community leader. She served on Canton's Select Board and is currently Chair of the Canton Housing Authority and the Affordable Housing Trust.

## Site Purpose

The campaign website has three primary jobs:

1. **Build credibility** — establish Lisa as a qualified, community-rooted candidate through her biography and track record
2. **Drive action** — convert visitors to volunteers and donors with clear CTAs
3. **Communicate platform** — give voters a clear picture of the issues she'll fight for on Beacon Hill

## Key Issues

- Housing and affordability
- Climate and clean energy
- Civil rights and immigration
- Democracy and civic access
- Education and opportunity

## Primary Audiences

- Canton, Stoughton, and Avon residents likely to vote in 2026
- Current and potential volunteers
- Potential donors
- Press and endorsers

## Conversion Hierarchy

1. Volunteer sign-up (`/get-involved.html`)
2. Donation (ActBlue link)
3. Social sharing / word of mouth
