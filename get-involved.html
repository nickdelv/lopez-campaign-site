How You Can Help

Every hour
moves the needle.

01
Knock Doors
The most effective way to reach voters is face-to-face. Join a canvassing shift in your town and have real conversations with your neighbors about what's at stake.
02
Make Calls
Can't get out to canvass? Phone banking from home is just as valuable. We'll give you a script and a list — you bring the energy and your local voice.
03
Host an Event
Invite a few neighbors over. Lisa will come to you. Living room conversations are how local campaigns build trust, one block at a time.
04
Spread the Word
Put a yard sign out front. Share on social media. Tell a friend who's on the fence. Visibility matters — especially in a local race decided by hundreds of votes.
